# Drift App - Marketing Campaign 2025

## Campaign Overview
**Goal:** Launch Drift meditation app to 15,000 members by Q2 2025
**Target Audience:** Wellness enthusiasts, meditation practitioners, eco-conscious individuals (18-45 years old)
**Platforms:** Instagram, TikTok
**Budget:** Organic content + Paid ads (optional)

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. **Daily Affirmations & Mindfulness** (40% of content)
- Share daily meditation tips
- Post motivational quotes from AI coaches (Maria & Zane)
- Behind-the-scenes of audiobook creation
- User testimonials and success stories

### 2. **Clean Water Mission** (30% of content)
- Impact stories: Show how donations help communities
- Monthly donation milestones
- Educational content about water crisis
- User voting on donation projects

### 3. **Product Features** (20% of content)
- Audiobook chapter highlights
- Sleep video recommendations
- Journal prompts and mood tracking
- New Year Calendar daily reveals

### 4. **Community & Engagement** (10% of content)
- User challenges (e.g., "7-day meditation challenge")
- Polls and interactive questions
- Member spotlights
- Live Q&A sessions with coaches

## Engagement Strategy

### Instagram
1. **Post Time:** 7-9 AM and 6-8 PM (optimal engagement)
2. **Frequency:** 5 posts/week (Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat)
3. **Stories:** 2-3 daily (behind-the-scenes, polls, Q&A)
4. **Reels:** 2x/week (trending audio + meditation content)
5. **Engagement:** Reply to all comments within 2 hours

### TikTok
1. **Post Time:** 6-9 AM and 7-11 PM (peak hours)
2. **Frequency:** 1-2 videos/day (consistency = algorithm boost)
3. **Trends:** Use trending sounds/challenges with meditation twist
4. **Duets/Stitches:** Engage with meditation/wellness creators
5. **Engagement:** Reply to comments with video responses

## Content Creation Checklist

### Before Posting
- [ ] Brand colors consistent (burgundy #8B4513, gold #D4AF37, beige #F5E6D3)
- [ ] Captions spell-checked
- [ ] Hashtags relevant and trending
- [ ] CTA clear (link in bio, download app, etc.)
- [ ] Image/video quality high (1080x1080 for Instagram, 9:16 for TikTok)

### Post-Publishing
- [ ] Pin important posts
- [ ] Monitor comments for first 2 hours
- [ ] Respond to DMs within 24 hours
- [ ] Track engagement metrics
- [ ] Share to Stories if high engagement

## Success Metrics

### Engagement
- Like rate: Target 5-8%
- Comment rate: Target 1-2%
- Share rate: Target 0.5-1%

### Growth
- Follower growth rate: 10-15% monthly
- Video views: Average 1,000-5,000 per TikTok
- Website clicks: 5-10% of followers

### Conversion
- App downloads: 5-10% of engaged audience
- Premium conversions: 10-20% of app users
- Retention rate: 30-day active users 40%+
