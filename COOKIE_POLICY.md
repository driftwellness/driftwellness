# Cookie Policy

## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Drift ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). This policy should be read in conjunction with our Privacy Policy.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website or use an application. Cookies allow the website or app to recognize your device and remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login status.

In addition to cookies, we may use other tracking technologies such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage to collect information about your use of the Service.

## Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

**Essential Cookies:** These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly. They enable core functionality such as user authentication, security, and session management. Without these cookies, certain features of the Service would not be available. These cookies do not collect personal information and cannot be disabled.

**Performance and Analytics Cookies:** These cookies help us understand how users interact with the Service by collecting information about which pages are visited, how long users spend on each page, and any error messages encountered. We use this information to improve the performance and user experience of the Service. These cookies collect aggregated and anonymized data.

**Functionality Cookies:** These cookies allow the Service to remember your preferences and settings, such as your language preference, theme selection, and notification settings. This enhances your user experience by providing a more personalized service.

**Advertising and Marketing Cookies:** We may use cookies to deliver targeted advertisements and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. These cookies track your browsing activity across different websites and apps to show you relevant ads. You can opt out of targeted advertising through your device settings or browser settings.

## Types of Cookies We Use

**Session Cookies:** These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser or app. They are used to maintain your session and ensure that you remain logged in as you navigate through the Service.

**Persistent Cookies:** These cookies remain on your device for a set period of time or until you delete them. They are used to remember your preferences and settings across multiple sessions.

**First-Party Cookies:** These cookies are set by Drift and are used to provide core functionality and improve your experience.

**Third-Party Cookies:** These cookies are set by third-party service providers, such as analytics providers (Google Analytics) and payment processors (Stripe). These third parties may use cookies to collect information about your activity across different websites and services.

## Third-Party Cookies and Services

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies on your device:

**Google Analytics:** We use Google Analytics to analyze how users interact with the Service. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information such as page views, session duration, and user demographics. This information is aggregated and anonymized. You can learn more about how Google uses data by visiting [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

**Stripe:** We use Stripe to process payments for premium subscriptions. Stripe may use cookies to facilitate secure payment processing and prevent fraud. You can learn more about Stripe's use of cookies by visiting [Stripe's Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy).

**Social Media Plugins:** If you choose to share content from Drift on social media platforms, those platforms may set cookies on your device. We do not control these cookies, and you should review the privacy policies of the respective social media platforms for more information.

## Managing Cookies

You have the right to accept or reject cookies. Most web browsers and mobile devices allow you to control cookies through their settings.

**Browser Settings:** You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies, accept only certain cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. Please note that if you disable cookies, some features of the Service may not function properly.

**Mobile Device Settings:** You can manage cookies and tracking technologies through your mobile device settings. On iOS devices, you can enable "Limit Ad Tracking" in the Privacy settings. On Android devices, you can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in the Google Settings app.

**Opt-Out Tools:** You can opt out of targeted advertising by visiting the following websites:

- [Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA)](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/)
- [Network Advertising Initiative (NAI)](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/)
- [European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance (EDAA)](http://www.youronlinechoices.eu/)

Please note that opting out of targeted advertising does not mean you will stop seeing ads; it means the ads you see will not be tailored to your interests.

## Do Not Track Signals

Some web browsers have a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you do not want to be tracked. Currently, there is no industry standard for how to respond to DNT signals, and we do not currently respond to DNT signals. We will continue to monitor developments in this area and may update our practices in the future.
