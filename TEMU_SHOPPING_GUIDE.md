## 🎯 TIPS FOR SUCCESS

**Quality Check:**
1. Order 1-2 samples first before bulk
2. Check reviews with photos
3. Look for 4+ star ratings
4. Read negative reviews to avoid issues

**Sizing:**
- Asian sizes run small - order 1 size up
- Check size chart in product description
- Read reviews about fit

**Colors:**
- Photos may differ from actual color
- Check reviews for color accuracy
- Stick to neutral tones (safer)

**Shipping:**
- Choose "Standard" (free, 1-2 weeks)
- Or "Express" ($5-10, 5-7 days)
- Track your order in Temu app
