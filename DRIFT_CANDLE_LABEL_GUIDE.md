# DRIFT Candle Label Design Guide

## 📐 Label Specifications

### **Size Options:**
- **Small (4oz jar):** 2.5" wide × 2" tall (6.4cm × 5cm)
- **Medium (8oz jar):** 3" wide × 2.5" tall (7.6cm × 6.4cm)
- **Large (12oz jar):** 3.5" wide × 3" tall (8.9cm × 7.6cm)

---

## 🎨 Design Elements

### **Style 1: Minimalist Cream (for Beige & Gold candles)**
- **Background:** Cream/beige (#F5E6D3)
- **Text:** "DRIFT" in serif font
- **Color:** Burgundy (#8B4049) or Gold (#D4AF37)
- **Layout:** Centered, elegant spacing

### **Style 2: Luxury Dark (for Burgundy & Terracotta candles)**
- **Background:** Dark charcoal/black (#2C2C2C)
- **Text:** "DRIFT" in serif font
- **Color:** Gold (#D4AF37)
- **Layout:** Centered, elegant spacing

---

## 🖨️ How to Print & Apply

### **Materials Needed:**
1. **Sticker paper** (matte or glossy)
   - Avery 22804 (weatherproof)
   - Online Labels OL177WX (waterproof)
2. **Printer** (inkjet or laser)
3. **Scissors** or label cutter

### **Printing Instructions:**
1. Download label template (see below)
2. Open in Canva, Photoshop, or Word
3. Print on sticker paper
4. Cut to size
5. Apply to clean, dry glass jar

### **Application Tips:**
- Clean jar with rubbing alcohol first
- Apply from center outward to avoid bubbles
- Smooth with credit card or squeegee
- Let dry 24 hours before handling
