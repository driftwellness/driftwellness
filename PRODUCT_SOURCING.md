## 🕯️ Luxury Scented Candles

### Option 1: Alibaba Custom Candles
- **URL:** https://www.alibaba.com/showroom/wholesale-custom-candles.html
- **Features:** Private labeling, custom colors (beige, burgundy, terracotta, gold)
- **MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity):** Usually 100-500 units
- **Est. Cost:** $2-5 per candle
- **Sell Price:** 299 kr (~$28)
- **Margin:** ~85%

### Option 2: Roam Homegrown (Private Label)
- **URL:** https://roamhomegrownwholesale.com/collections/private-label-candle-collections-1
- **Features:** Luxury custom candles, low minimums, quick turnaround
- **MOQ:** 50-100 units
- **Est. Cost:** $5-8 per candle
- **Sell Price:** 299 kr (~$28)
- **Margin:** ~75%

### Option 3: Faire Marketplace
- **URL:** https://www.faire.com/discover/no-lable-candles
- **Features:** No minimum orders, variety of suppliers
- **MOQ:** 6-12 units per order
- **Est. Cost:** $8-12 per candle
- **Sell Price:** 299 kr (~$28)
- **Margin:** ~60%

**RECOMMENDATION:** Start with Faire (low risk, no MOQ), then scale to Alibaba for better margins.

## 📊 Summary & Next Steps

### Recommended Starting Strategy:

**Phase 1: Low-Risk Start (Total Investment: ~$2,000-3,000)**
- Faire: Candles (12 units), Ankh necklaces (12 units)
- Bulk Apothecary: Essential oils (20 sets)
- Pravacana: Yoga mats (25 units with Drift logo)
- Great Eastern: Meditation cushions (12 units)
- Faire: Yoga clothing (12 tops, 12 pants)

**Phase 2: Scale Up (When you have sales)**
- Move to Alibaba for candles, jewelry, clothing (better margins)
- Increase inventory based on best-sellers
- Add kimonos and premium items

### Profit Margins:
- **Candles:** 60-85%
- **Essential Oils:** 30-50%
- **Yoga Mats:** 60-75%
- **Meditation Cushions:** 40-55%
- **Ankh Necklaces:** 60-85%
- **Yoga Clothing:** 45-80%

**Average Margin: ~60%**
